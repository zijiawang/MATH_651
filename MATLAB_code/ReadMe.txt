1. Call_options_prices_heston.m and Call_options_prices_jumps.m are matlab codes to get the estimated prices under heston model and jumps model respectively. The input in these codes are the estimated parameters given by MCMC simulation.

2. CLM.txt and CLJ.txt are the estimated prices and market prices of CLM16 options and CLJ16 options under different strikes.

3. Options_Prices.m is for plotting the market prices and estimated prices, and calculate the corresponding SSE.
